Elon Musk

About

Elon Musk was born June, 28, 1971. He is a South-African born Canadian-American engineer and inventor. Elon Musk Co-Founded Paypal and online banking website X.com. PayPal was bought by eBay for $1.5 billion and Elon would later on start his own space company that same year, SpaceX. Fastforward in 2008, Elon Musk became the largest shareholder in a company called Tesla Motors, which would put him to serve as its chairman and CEO. Since that time, both companies have rolled out 5 different consumer vehicles, and have been in the process of developing Semi trucks. SpaceX specifically has been the first privatly funded liquid-propellant rocket to launch into Earth's orbit. It also is the first space company to recover a spacecraft and first private space company to send a spacecraft to the International Space Station.
